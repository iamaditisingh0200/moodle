What is a Computer?

Date: 29 November 2025 | Author: Admin

A computer is an electronic device that accepts data, processes it according to a set of instructions (called programs), and gives meaningful output. It can store data, perform calculations, and help in almost every field like education, health, banking, and entertainment.

What is Programming?

Date: 29 November 2025 | Author: Admin

Programming is the process of writing instructions for a computer to perform specific tasks. These instructions are written in programming languages like C, C++, Java, Python, and JavaScript. A program tells the computer what to do step by step.
